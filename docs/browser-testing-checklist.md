# Browser Testing Checklist

## Student flow
- Register a student account.
- Log in and access the dashboard.
- Open a lesson module and view the content.
- Complete the immediate recall quiz.
- Submit the NASA-TLX survey.
- Verify immediate results are shown.
- Wait for the delayed window and complete the delayed quiz.
- Review delayed results.

## Admin flow
- Log in as the seeded admin.
- Open the dashboard.
- Review modules, quizzes, and participant data.
- Assign groups to participants.
- Review the analytics dashboard.
- Export results as CSV.
